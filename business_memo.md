
# Business Memo

## Objective

Reduce customer churn through targeted retention campaigns and customer engagement strategies.

## Key Findings

1. Customers with high recency are more likely to churn.
2. Customers with lower website engagement show higher churn risk.
3. Loyal and high-value customers contribute significant revenue.
4. Support ticket dissatisfaction may negatively impact retention.
5. Dormant customers require re-engagement campaigns.

## Recommendations

### For At-Risk Customers
- Send personalized offers.
- Launch win-back email campaigns.
- Provide special discounts.

### For Loyal Customers
- Offer loyalty rewards.
- Provide exclusive product access.
- Encourage referrals.

### For Dormant Customers
- Conduct feedback surveys.
- Run reactivation campaigns.
- Offer limited-time promotions.

## Expected Business Impact

Implementing targeted retention strategies can improve customer retention, increase lifetime value, and reduce revenue loss due to churn.
